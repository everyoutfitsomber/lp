Our Teeny Tiny Bezels

What is a bezel?

It's the small ring of metal surrounding the outside of a gemstone. It holds the gemstone in place.

Why do we love bezels?

We love bezels for their minimalism and their symmetry. They are our favorite way to set a gemstone.

What's the challenge with bezels?

Most jewelry is cast via molten metal. The cylindrical wall of a bezel setting has to be thick enough for the molten metal to flow into.

This means that, owing to the process by which they are made, the bezels on small stud earrings have always looked a bit "chunky".

Worse, it means there is less space for a gemstone. A smaller gemstone has to be used, leading to a more lucklustre sparkle.

How did we solve this?

We've championed a special process that allows us to create ultra-thin bezels.

It took us 7 months.

But it was worth it.

It means that (1) less of the bezel overlaps the stone, and (2) we can use a larger gemstone than anybody else for the same-size earring.

It's a trade secret of ours, and we're yet to find another jewelry brand who does what we do.

It means that we have an unfair advantage in sparkle.

For you, it means endless "They're so small and sparkly!" compliments.

Ultra-Hypoallergenic By Design

Made for the most sensitive ears for all-day wear.

1. The metal we use is special

We source a special alloy more-often used in luxury watches than in traditional jewelry.

Not only is this alloy ultra-hypollergenic, exceeding the highest EU standards - one of the reasons for its use in Swiss watches - it stands up perfectly to the special process we use to create our ultra-thin bezels (gold and silver being far too soft).

2. The gold coating we use is special

Contrary to what many people think, most nickel sensitivities in jewelry are triggered not by the metal it is made from, but by a thin nickel coating applied just prior to the gold coating process.

This nickel layer is applied because it helps the final gold coating process, but it causes havoc for any ears with a nickel sensitivity.

We do not use normal coating methods for this reason.

We use a much rarer coating process that allows us to eliminate the nickel layer altogether.

In a nutshell - we hear from customers all the time that our earrings are the only ones they can wear all day long.

About us

Who we are

We're a tiny team of artisans who work from our cozy Studio at the base of the Rocky Mountains in Colorado.

Small dainty earrings is our gig. If that's you, you've found your home!

Tiny is mighty

Being small affords us one special privilege -

We get to work deeply and purposefully on one design at a time, for as long as it takes.

There's no pressure on us to build out a vast collection in order to sustain ourselves. Creatively, we find that very liberating.

The art of craftmanship

Once we realized that an exceptional small round bezel stud earring was next-to-impossible to come by, we had the luxury of being able to spend 7 months bringing it to life, and in the process learn new and interesting ways of crafting jewelry.

We love to tinker and draw on other fields for inspiration - for example, we were able to borrow a technique from the luxury Swiss watch world to create our famous leaf-thin bezels.

100% privately-owned

We're 100% privately-owned with no outside investors or influences. We get to work on what we want, for as long as we want.

Tiny footprint

We're fortunate to be just down the street from the world's leading experts in eco-friendly packaging. In consultation with them we've been able to eliminate all plastic from our packaging, and we have a suspicion that we have the lowest carbon- and eco-footprint of any jewelry company in the United States.

Shipped from Colorado

We pack all our jewelry ourselves and ship direct from our Studio here in Colorado.

Our voice

We're small, we don't have a marketing department. Any email you receive from us is written in the first-person by our co-founder Annie. You can reply to her any time and she'll write back.
